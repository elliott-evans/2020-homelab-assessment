My Hardware

Lancelot

Lancelot is the foremost system in my homelab. He runs on an IBM System x3550 M4 platform. With Lancelot, I can (normally) host websites, transfer lots of files between different systems, manage my other servers, or even help to cure cancer! That's right, Lancelot can do it all.
